About Our World of Text

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Our World of Text is an infinite grid of text editable by any visitor. The changes made by other people appear on your screen as they happen. Everyone starts in the same place, but you can scroll through the world using your mouse.

Put any letters at the end of the URL to go to a new world. For example, {{ website }}forexample. They all start off blank. You can also create a custom world for you and your friends.

This is a recreation of YWOT from scratch.
The subreddit is at /r/OurWorldOfText.
We also have a Discord server.

Contact OWOT: ourworldoftext@gmail.com
